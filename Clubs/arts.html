About

Welcome to the green heartbeat of our campus – the College Eco Club, where awareness meets action and every step counts towards a sustainable future.

Introducing the Eco Club of the Department – a passionate community committed to environmental stewardship, sustainability, and spreading eco-consciousness. Whether you're an environmental enthusiast or a curious learner, our club offers a platform to connect, act, and inspire change.

Here's an overview:

  • Tree Plantation Drives
  • Cleanliness & Recycling Campaigns
  • Nature Walks & Eco-Talks

Join our Eco Club and be part of a movement that nurtures not just nature, but also minds and mindsets. Whether you're ready to lead a cleanup drive, raise your voice for the planet, or simply adopt greener habits, this is your place. Let’s grow greener, together!
