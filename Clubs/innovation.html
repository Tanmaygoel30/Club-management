About

We, at the Entrepreneurship Cell, share the common belief that India will only emerge as a world power if the youth breaks the shackles of prejudices and inertia that has kept them from starting up. Creating an entrepreneurial ecosystem to provide a platform to individuals with creative minds, ideas and the potential to run a substantial business, is what we strive towards continually

What We Do:

  1. Workshops and Training
  2. Idea Incubation
  3. Hackathons and Challenges
  4. Guest Lectures and Talks
  5. Networking and Community
